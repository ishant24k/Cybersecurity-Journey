# TryHackMe – Network Fundamentals: (Extending Your Network)

## What is Network Extension?
Using devices and techniques to expand the size, range, and capabilities of a network.

## Key Points:
- **Switches** – Connect devices in a LAN
- **Repeaters** – Boost signal strength
- **VLANs** – Segment networks for security

## Example:
Using an access point to extend Wi-Fi coverage in a large building.

## What I Learned:
- VLANs improve both performance and security.
